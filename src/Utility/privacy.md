At PlannedRations, your privacy matters. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our services.

## 1. Introduction

PlannedRations (“we,” “our,” or “us”) is committed to protecting your privacy.  
By using our website, mobile app, or related services, you agree to the terms described in this policy.  
If you do not agree, please discontinue use of our services.

## 2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

- **Personal Information:** Name, email address, phone number, and account details provided during sign-up or profile creation.  
- **Usage Data:** Information about how you use the app (meal plans, recipes saved, grocery list interactions).  
- **Device & Technical Data:** IP address, browser type, operating system, mobile device information, and app usage analytics.  
- **Payment Data:** Billing details when you subscribe to premium plans. Payment processing is handled securely through third-party providers.  
- **Optional Health & Lifestyle Data:** Diet preferences, nutrition goals, allergies, or fitness information — only if you choose to share.

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

- Provide and improve our services (meal planning, grocery lists, nutrition tracking).  
- Personalize recommendations based on your preferences and pantry.  
- Send important updates, newsletters, or promotional content (you can opt out anytime).  
- Process payments and manage subscriptions.  
- Ensure security, prevent fraud, and maintain service integrity.

## 4. Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal data. We may share limited information in these cases:

- **Service Providers:** Trusted partners who help us operate our platform (e.g., payment processors, analytics tools).  
- **Legal Obligations:** If required by law, regulation, or government request.  
- **Business Transfers:** In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, user data may be transferred.

## 5. Data Retention

We retain your information only as long as necessary to provide services or comply with legal obligations.  
You may request deletion of your account and data at any time.

## 6. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights:

- **Access:** Request a copy of the information we hold about you.  
- **Correction:** Ask us to update or fix inaccurate details.  
- **Deletion:** Request that we delete your personal data.  
- **Portability:** Request a copy of your data in a portable format.  
- **Opt-Out:** Choose not to receive marketing communications.

## 7. Data Security

We use appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data, including encryption, secure servers, and regular security reviews.  
However, no online service can guarantee 100% security.

## 8. Children’s Privacy

PlannedRations is **not designed for children under 13.**  
We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.  
Parents or guardians who believe their child has provided data should contact us immediately.

## 9. International Users

If you access PlannedRations from outside your home country, please note that your data may be transferred and processed in countries with different data protection laws.

## 10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time.  
Any significant changes will be communicated via email or app notification.  
The “Last Updated” date at the top will always show the most recent version.
